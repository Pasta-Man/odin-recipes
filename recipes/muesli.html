Muesli

Description

This recipe is for a delicious bowl of muesli mixed with your favorite fruit.

Ingredients

  • 2 handfuls of rolls oats
  • 1/2 handful of brown sugar
  • 2 dashes of cinnamon
  • 1 handful of raisins
  • 1 chopped banana
  • 1 handful of your favorite granola
  • Any other of your favorite fruit
  • Enough soy milk to cover the oats

Steps

  1. Mix together oats, brown sugar, and cinnamon into a bowl
  2. Toss in 1 handful of raisins, 1 chopped banana, and 1 handful of granola
  3. Add any of your favorite fruit
  4. Add just enough soy milk to cover the oats>
  5. Enjoy!
